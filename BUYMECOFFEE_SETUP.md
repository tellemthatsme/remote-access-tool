# BuyMeACoffee Setup Guide

## Step 1: Create Account (2 min)

1. Go to **https://www.buymeacoffee.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Sign up with:
   - Google account, OR
   - Email + password

---

## Step 2: Create Your Page (3 min)

1. After login, click **"Create a page"**

2. Fill in your page details:

```
Page Name/Username: remote-pc

This will become: buymeacoffee.com/remote-pc
```

3. Click **"Continue"**

---

## Step 6: Get Your Link

1. Click **"Publish page"**
2. Copy your page URL:
   ```
   https://buymeacoffee.com/YOUR_USERNAME
   ```

---

## Step 7: Update Landing Page

Edit `landing.html` and replace:
```html
<a href="https://www.buymeacoffee.com/YOUR_LINK_HERE" class="btn">
```

With your actual link!
